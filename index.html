Client Challenge

  • Largest increase in US wage and price inflation in 40 years: Inflation rates above 7.0% for the past three months.
  • US pricing pressures have spread across more than 90% of consumer spending categories
  • US post-COVID private sector wages rising more than ~2x pre-COVID rates
  • Likely inflationary environment through 2023 – potentially longer

Our Solution

Rapidly understand potential impact of inflationary risks to the enterprise.

Develop clear and actionable path to insulating the enterprise from major inflation, volatility and disruption risks by deploying best-in-class approaches and capabilities – to prepare the organizations for this new normal

Insulating from Inflation IDP Offering

Our approach targets all major inflation and volatility risks through a coordinated deployment of 6 functional workstreams

Main inflation, volatility and disruption effects…
… require a coordinated, cross-functional approach involving all enterprise functions

Cost increase

How much has your unit cost increased? How much has your operational cost as % revenue increased?

Revenue reduction

Are your customers going away because of price increases? Do you have ability to preserve margin through price?

Supply instability

Are you experiencing delays on shipments and/or scarcity of products?

Talent shortage

Are you experiencing labor shortage internally and externally (e.g., contractors)?

Performance management

Are you setting targets based on informed future-looking assessment. Which systems and tools will you need to assure future resilience?
